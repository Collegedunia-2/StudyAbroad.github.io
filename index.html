How much funds will I need to study abroad?
The total funding required for your complete process can be bifurcated into process charges (application fee & visa fee), cost of living & tuition fee. Usually, every country has an approx. cost of living but the tuition fee for every University will vary. We can provide options as low as ₹20,00,000 inclusive of all costs. We also provide Financial Assistance for the same in the form of loans through our partners & various scholarships.
What is the service fee that Collegedunia - Study Abroad charges for doing an application?

The process through Collegedunia – Study Abroad is completely FREE, right from profile evaluation, Shortlisting, application process and Visa process. In fact, we offer Visa Scholarships of up to ₹40,000 covering the Embassy visa application fees.

How many University/College options can I apply through Collegedunia?

We have more than 5000 Universities & Colleges with which we are partnered. These are spread across the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, New Zealand and Ireland.

What is the total time taken to receive an offer letter?

An offer letter can be received in as low as 7 days, and in some cases can take as long as 2 months. At Collegedunia Study Abroad, we typically provide an offer letter within 21 days across all countries, which is well above industry standards.
